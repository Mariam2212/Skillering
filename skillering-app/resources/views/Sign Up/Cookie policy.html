Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device and remember your preferences and activities.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. Cookies may also be used for security and login functionality.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., login, security).
  • Performance Cookies: Collect data about how visitors use our website.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences and settings.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads based on your interests.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may come from third-party services (such as Google Analytics or social media plugins) to provide analytics or advertising features.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.
